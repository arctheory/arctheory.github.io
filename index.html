01 Executive Summary

Introduction

The global 2D Chromatography market size is expected to grow from USD 33.4 million in 2020 to USD 48.5 million by 2026 at a CAGR of 6.4%.

The ongoing COVID-19 pandemic has increased the adoption levels of 2D chromatography in various applications. Many researchers are aggressively using the concept of 2D chromatography to understand the gene structure of Sars-CoV-2 and to develop preventive measures. Considering the fact aforementioned, the global 2D Chromatography market is forecasted to register better growth statistics during the forecast period.

The growth of this market is driven largely by factors such as pollution reduction programs, R&D investment by pharma companies, government support, and the growing petrol industry. Y-O-Y growth in the number of research activities happening across the world is expected to encourage the growth of the market.

On the other hand, the scarcity of human resources who handle the 2D chromatography operations is predicted to hamper the growth rate of the market during the forecast period.

02 Market Overview

Defination & Scope
Currency, Year & Forecast

Virtual data rooms are majorly cloud-based platforms for sharing of critical documents among multiple stakeholders in a secured and controlled environment. These platforms are often used during mergers & acquisitions (M&A) processes to accelerate the due diligence tasks. The key stakeholders include business brokers, government contractors, financial advisors, real estate firms, private equity firms, and venture capitalists. According to the industry experts, virtual data rooms have been successful in reducing the M&A processing time approximately by four times than traditional data rooms.

Ever-Followed Strategy of M&A

The prominent regions of the world, North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific have been consistent in terms of number of M&A deals across sectors. M&A and partnerships are key strategies adopted by investors and big companies to generate more revenue and innovate on their product/service offerings. Carrying out M&A transaction through traditional methods often takes more than one month of time while virtual data room software platforms reduces this processing time by 4 times. The key capability of virtual data room software to allow multiple stakeholders efficiently and securely collaborate with each other during the M&A process makes it a necessary tool for enterprises and investment firms.

Cloud Technology for Virtual Data Rooms

Cloud computing is the key enabler for virtual data room. It allows the stakeholders involved in an M&A process to coordinate with each other from any place at any time. Due to the flexibility, scalability, and anytime-anywhere access features of the cloud, most of the virtual data room vendors offer their products through cloud deployment models.

06 By Region

North America accounted for the largest share of the virtual data room market in 2019, followed by Europe. This can primarily be attributed to factors such as the increasing applications of virtual data room in highly competitive market in America where large number of multinational companies are aggressively involved in conducting various business strategies.

On the other hand, the Asia Pacific market is projected to grow at a lucrative rate throughout the forecast period. The anticipated growth can be attributed to the Asian companies that are striving to boost their industry growth and revenue generation in their individual market wherein need for file sharing for various deals are crucial.

The market studied is expected to grow at the fastest pace in Asia-Pacific. The rising focus of the major market players is on delivering advanced, efficient virtual data room solution integrated with cutting edge technology to the growing business countries, in order to cater to the need for file sharing. In addition, the increasing adoption of virtual data room platform is boosted by enterprise increased investment of the merger, acquisitions, partnership, collaboration for product development, is expected to drive the Asian market.

07 North America

The countries covered in North America are the US and Canada. The region is the largest contributor in the global virtual data room market. From 2014 to 2019, the region has recorded number of M&A deals in the range of 14 thousand to 19 thousand every year. The highest number of M&A deals acts as a major driving factor for the virtual data room market in the North American region.

In North America, the US occupied significant share of virtual data room market, because of the increasing adoption of virtual data room through diverse governments and companies. The rising demand for easy and secure strategies for the storage of essential information and the increasing need for transparent and efficient data sharing alternatives are fueling the market growth. Furthermore, the developing fundraising, rising range of mergers and acquisitions, and joint ventures with some of the distinguished gamers have fueled the market throughout the region.

The innovation and development in the region is also expected to offer growth to the market. For example, one newer company, named Deal Room, has currently emerged as a virtual data room software program provider. The software not only consists of traditional digital data room features, but also on line data storage and task management capabilities.
